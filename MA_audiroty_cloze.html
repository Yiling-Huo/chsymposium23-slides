Using the cloze task to index predictability

The N400: a negatively-going ERP component that peaks around 400ms after stimulus onset. In language, usually taken to index lexical access.

Using the cloze probability measure, Kutas and colleagues showed that congruent sentence completions elicited N400s whose amplitudes scaled inversely with their cloze probability. (N400 amplitude: 90% cloze < 70% cloze < 30% cloze) (Kutas and Hillyard, 1984; Kutas et al., 1984).

Tone sandhi in Mandarin Chinese

Lexical tone: pitch contour that encodes meaning.

ma1 "mother", ma2 "hemp", ma3 "horse", ma4 "scold"

ma1ma1-ma4-ma3 "Mom scolds the horse."

Tone sandhi in Mandarin Chinese

Tone sandhi: lexical tone can undergo change triggered by neighboring linguistic environments.

The yi sandhi: numeral yi1 "one"

yi4tian1, yi4tiao2, yi4ba3, yi2ge4

The T3 sandhi: numeral liang3 "two"

xiao3gou3 → xiao2gou3

Tone sandhi in prediction

Some evidence that show native listeners can use the T3 sandhi to predict (Chow & Huo, 2020).

Some evidence that listeners also predict using the yi sandhi when speech rate is slow (Liu & Wang, 2023)

But results aren't always replicated...
